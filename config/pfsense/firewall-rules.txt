[SECTION 3: STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION FOR HIRING MANAGERS]
================================================================================
- Network Isolation: The Kali Linux attacker node (192.168.100.200) is locked down 
  via layer-3 rules to prevent any pivoting or lateral movement out of the lab network into 
  home production zones[cite: 296, 388].
- Controlled Egress: Absolute internet lockout is applied to the Windows 10 Victim 
  and Domain Controller to prevent uncontrolled malware beaconing, tracking, or unexpected updates 
  during live attack simulations[cite: 296].
